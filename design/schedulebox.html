Schedule Box  - A Unique Task Management App
What is it?

Schedule Box is an Android app which can help users manage the tasks they have. It provides users with several ways to create a schedule and make a more reasonable management of their time.

I am the independent designer and developer of this Android app. Before my design process, I first constructed a user research among my classmates. With such study, I found out several common pain points of them on the topic of time management. Then I start my journey to create a more useful application to help them make a better schedule of their day.

What problems does it solve?

In most cases, we know what we should do and indeed there are so many things on our to-do list to occupize our time. But actually we don't know how to plan the sequence of all the tasks we are supposed to deal with, in order to have a better task performance. With the difficulty dgree you set up for each task in the to-do list, this app can help you to organize them in order more conveniently and scientifically.

How does it work?

  • Log in with student ID and search for the courses you are interested in.


  • Basic information for a course and the feedback from students who have taken the course.


  • Score a course you have taken and leave comments for it.
